To play the game type in commands:

  • Go [direction] OR [direction]: move
  • Take [item]: get an item
  • Use [item]: do something with item
  • Use [item] on [second item]: use an item with another item
  • look: get description of current area
  • look at [item] OR look [item] OR examine [item]: get description of a particular item
  • inventory OR i: list inventory
  • drop [item]: drops item on ground
  • break [item]: attempt to break the item with your wrench
